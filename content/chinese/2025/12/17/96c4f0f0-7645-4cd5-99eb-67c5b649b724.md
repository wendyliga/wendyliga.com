Once the dough was ready, they chatted while wrapping the black sesame filling into the dough, making small round balls. Finally, they put the dumplings into a pot and cooked them on low heat. Soon, a sweet aroma filled the air, and the dumplings gradually floated to the surface of the water.

When the dumplings were ready, they sat down and tasted the soft and sweet soup dumplings together. Xiaoming said, “The Winter Solstice Festival is not only about eating dumplings but also a great time to gather with family.” Lili nodded and said, “Tomorrow, we can tell Grandpa and Grandma the story of the Winter Solstice to make them even happier!”

- 吃汤圆 (chī tāngyuán) – To eat dumplings
- 讲故事 (jiǎng gùshì) – To tell a story

### Resultative Complements:
- 做好 (zuò hǎo) – Finished making something
- 煮开 (zhǔ kāi) – To boil something until it is ready
- 漂出 (piāo chū) – To float out

### Expressing Simultaneous Actions:

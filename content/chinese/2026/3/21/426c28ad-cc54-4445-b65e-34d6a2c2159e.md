## Grammatical Points and Analysis

### 把 (bǎ) Construction:
- **把鸡蛋放在桌子上** — "把 (bǎ)" is used to move the object before the verb, emphasizing the action done to the egg. It highlights what happens to the egg as a result of the action.
- **把鸡蛋立起来** — Same structure; "立起来" is the result of what is done to the egg using 把.

### Resultative Complements:
- **立起来** (lì qǐlái) — "起来" indicates the egg achieves the upright position as a result of the action.
- **走进来** (zǒu jìnlái) — "进来" shows the direction and result of Lili's walking action (coming inside).
- **成功了** (chénggōng le) — "了" marks a completed result; success has been achieved.

### 越来越 (yuè lái yuè) Pattern:
- **白天会越来越长** — "越来越 + adjective" means "more and more." The days will become longer and longer.
- **天气也会越来越暖和** — The weather will also get warmer and warmer.

### 不但…也… (bùdàn… yě…) Structure:
- **不但学会了一个有趣的习俗，也更了解了大自然的美丽** — This is a correlative conjunction meaning "not only… but also…" used to connect two positive outcomes.

### Verb-Object Phrases:
- **深呼吸了一下** — "Deep breath" as a verb-object phrase; "了一下" softens the action, meaning "took one breath."
- **拍了一张照片** — "拍照片" means to take a photo; "一张" uses the measure word 张 for flat objects like photos.
- **发给妈妈看** — A serial verb construction: "send (it) to Mom to look at." Two verbs in sequence sharing the same subject.

### 一样 (yīyàng) Comparison:
- **白天和黑夜一样长** — "A 和 B 一样 + adjective" means "A and B are equally…" Here daytime and nighttime are equally long.

### 要 (yào) for Necessity or Intention:
- **你要非常慢慢地放** — "要" here expresses what one must or needs to do, indicating instruction or requirement.
- **他决定每年春分都要再试一次** — "要" expresses intention or a firm decision to do something.

五一劳动节快到了。小明和丽丽是好朋友，他们的家人计划一起去旅行。

星期六早上，小明很早就起床了。他非常高兴，因为这是他第一次和丽丽的家人一起出去玩。他先吃了早饭，然后去丽丽家。

小明到了丽丽家，丽丽已经在客厅里等他了。桌子上放着一张很大的地图，旁边还有很多零食和水果。

小明说："丽丽，你已经准备好了吗？我们今天要去乡下看花田，我真的太开心了！"

丽丽笑着说："我也是！我已经收拾好行李了。你看，我准备了面包、饼干、苹果，还有几瓶水。我们路上可以吃。"

小明说："太好了！那我们先看看地图吧，我想知道怎么走。"

他们两个人一起看地图，找到了去乡下的路线。丽丽用笔在地图上画了一条线，这样他们就不会走错路了。

可是，过了一会儿，小明想去洗手间。他站起来的时候，不小心把地图碰到了地上。他没有看到，就走了。等他回来的时候，地图不见了。

小明着急地说："丽丽，地图呢？我刚才还看到它在桌子上，现在怎么找不到了？"

丽丽也找了找，但是没有找到。原来，丽丽的小猫跑过来，把地图从地上拖到了沙发下面，地图被弄坏了。

小明很难过，他说："都是我不好，是我不小心把地图弄丢了。现在我们怎么办？没有地图，我们怎么找到路？"

丽丽想了想，然后笑着对小明说："别担心！我们可以用手机查地图啊。手机上的地图比纸地图还方便呢，它可以告诉我们怎么走，还能告诉我们要多长时间。"

小明听了，觉得丽丽说得对。他拿出手机，打开了地图。他们很快就找到了去花田的路线。

这时候，丽丽的爸爸妈妈也准备好了。爸爸把行李放到车上，妈妈带了一些好吃的饭菜。全家人都上了车。

在路上，大家一边吃零食，一边看窗外的风景。天气非常好，蓝蓝的天上有白白的云。路两边有很多绿色的树和漂亮的花。

大约过了两个小时，他们终于到了乡下的花田。小明一下车就跑了过去。花田里有红色的、黄色的、紫色的花，非常漂亮。风一吹，花儿就像在跳舞一样。

丽丽也跑过来，高兴地说："太美了！我们拍张照片吧！"

小明和丽丽在花田里拍了很多照片，大家都玩得很开心。

回家的路上，小明对丽丽说："谢谢你今天帮我想办法。以后出去玩，我一定会更小心的。"

丽丽笑着说："没关系，朋友就是要互相帮助的嘛。下次我们再一起出来玩吧！"

那一天，大家都过得非常快乐。这真是一个美好的五一劳动节。

May Day Labor Day is almost here. Xiao Ming and Lili are good friends, and their families plan to travel together.

On Saturday morning, Xiao Ming got up very early. He was very happy because this was his first time going out with Lili's family. He first ate breakfast, then went to Lili's house.

When Xiao Ming arrived at Lili's house, Lili was already waiting for him in the living room. On the table there was a very big map, and beside it there were many snacks and fruits.

Xiao Ming said: "Lili, are you already ready? We're going to the countryside today to see the flower fields. I'm really so happy!"

Lili said with a smile: "Me too! I've already packed the luggage. Look, I prepared bread, biscuits, apples, and also several bottles of water. We can eat them on the way."

Xiao Ming said: "Great! Then let's look at the map first. I want to know how to get there."

The two of them looked at the map together and found the route to the countryside. Lili used a pen to draw a line on the map, so they wouldn't take the wrong road.

But after a while, Xiao Ming wanted to go to the bathroom. When he stood up, he accidentally knocked the map onto the floor. He didn't notice and just walked away. When he came back, the map was gone.

Xiao Ming said anxiously: "Lili, where's the map? I just saw it on the table a moment ago. How come I can't find it now?"

Lili also looked around, but didn't find it. It turned out that Lili's little cat had run over, dragged the map from the floor to under the sofa, and the map was ruined.

Xiao Ming was very sad. He said: "It's all my fault. I was careless and lost the map. What do we do now? Without a map, how can we find the way?"

Lili thought for a moment, then said to Xiao Ming with a smile: "Don't worry! We can use our phones to look up the map. The map on the phone is even more convenient than a paper map. It can tell us how to go, and it can also tell us how long it will take."

Xiao Ming listened and felt that Lili was right. He took out his phone and opened the map. They quickly found the route to the flower fields.

At this time, Lili's mom and dad were also ready. Dad put the luggage in the car, and Mom brought some delicious food. The whole family got in the car.

On the way, everyone ate snacks while looking at the scenery outside the window. The weather was very nice — the blue sky had white clouds. On both sides of the road there were many green trees and beautiful flowers.

After about two hours, they finally arrived at the flower fields in the countryside. As soon as Xiao Ming got out of the car, he ran over. The flower fields had red, yellow, and purple flowers — very beautiful. When the wind blew, the flowers looked like they were dancing.

Lili also ran over and said happily: "So beautiful! Let's take a photo!"

Xiao Ming and Lili took many photos in the flower fields, and everyone had a great time.

On the way home, Xiao Ming said to Lili: "Thank you for helping me think of a solution today. In the future when we go out to play, I will definitely be more careful."

Lili said with a smile: "It's okay. Friends are supposed to help each other. Let's go out together again next time!"

That day, everyone had a very happy time. It was truly a wonderful May Day Labor Day.

## Grammatical Points and Analysis

### Verb-Object Phrases

- **起床** (qǐchuáng) — to get up (literally: rise + bed)
- **收拾行李** (shōushi xíngli) — to pack luggage
- **吃早饭** (chī zǎofàn) — to eat breakfast
- **想办法** (xiǎng bànfǎ) — to think of a solution
- **拍照片** (pāi zhàopiàn) — to take photos

### Resultative Complements

- **准备好** (zhǔnbèi hǎo) — to be done preparing; 好 indicates completion
- **找到** (zhǎodào) — to find (successfully); 到 indicates the action reached its result
- **收拾好** (shōushi hǎo) — to be done packing; 好 indicates completion
- **弄丢** (nòng diū) — to lose (something) by accident; 丢 is the result of the action
- **弄坏** (nòng huài) — to break/ruin (something); 坏 is the result

### Key Sentence Patterns

- **快……了** (kuài…le) — "about to / almost": 五一劳动节**快到了** — May Day is almost here.
- **先……然后……** (xiān…ránhòu…) — "first…then…": 他**先**吃了早饭，**然后**去丽丽家。
- **一边……一边……** (yìbiān…yìbiān…) — "while doing X, also doing Y": 大家**一边**吃零食，**一边**看窗外的风景。
- **把 (bǎ) structure** — used to show disposal or result of an action on a specific object: 小明不小心**把**地图碰到了地上。
- **被 (bèi) structure** — passive voice: 地图**被**弄坏了 — The map was ruined.
- **比 (bǐ) comparison** — used for comparing: 手机上的地图**比**纸地图还方便。
- **像……一样** (xiàng…yíyàng) — "like / as if": 花儿就**像**在跳舞**一样**。
- **原来** (yuánlái) — "it turned out that": **原来**，丽丽的小猫跑过来……
- **都是我不好** (dōu shì wǒ bù hǎo) — "It's all my fault" — a common expression for self-blame.

### Useful Adverbs and Time Words

- **已经……了** (yǐjīng…le) — "already": 我**已经**收拾好行李**了**。
- **终于** (zhōngyú) — "finally": 他们**终于**到了乡下的花田。
- **大约** (dàyuē) — "approximately": **大约**过了两个小时。

## 汉字

**小鸟的故事**

春天到了，天气变得越来越暖和。小明是一个八岁的男孩，他每天都在学校认真学习。他有一个姐姐叫丽丽，丽丽比他大两岁，她是一个非常善良的女孩。姐弟俩的关系很好，常常一起玩。

有一天下午，小明放学以后，走到学校的花园里。花园里有很多漂亮的花，红色的、黄色的、白色的，非常好看。小明一边走一边看花。突然，他听到了一个很小的声音。他停下来，仔细地听了听。那个声音是从一棵大树下面传来的。

小明慢慢地走过去，发现了一只小鸟。那只小鸟的翅膀受伤了，不能飞。小鸟看起来又害怕又难过。小明觉得非常心疼，他想："我一定要帮助这只小鸟。"

小明小心地把小鸟放在手上，然后慢慢地走回家。回到家以后，丽丽看到了小鸟，马上跑过来。

丽丽问他："小明，这只小鸟是从哪里来的？它怎么了？"

小明回答说："姐姐，我在学校的花园里发现了它。它的翅膀受伤了，不能飞了。我们能不能帮助它？"

丽丽说："当然可以！我们一起想办法吧。"

丽丽找到了一个小盒子，在里面放了一些软的布和棉花。她把盒子做成了一个温暖的小窝。小明轻轻地把小鸟放进了窝里。小鸟终于不那么害怕了，安静地躺在温暖的窝里。

从那天开始，姐弟俩每天一起照顾小鸟。早上上学以前，小明会先给小鸟喂水。放学回家以后，丽丽会给小鸟准备一些食物，比如小虫子和米。每天晚上，他们会一起看看小鸟的翅膀有没有好一点。

一个星期过去了，小鸟的翅膀慢慢好了一些。两个星期以后，小鸟开始在盒子里动来动去。三个星期以后，小鸟已经能站起来了。小明和丽丽都非常高兴。

又过了几天，小鸟的翅膀完全好了。它在房间里飞来飞去，看起来非常开心。小明知道，小鸟应该回到天空去了。虽然他很舍不得，但是他明白小鸟属于大自然。

那天是一个晴天，阳光很好。姐弟俩一起把小鸟带到了花园里。小明打开手，小鸟看了看他，然后张开翅膀，飞向了蓝蓝的天空。

小明看着小鸟飞远了，眼睛有一点红。丽丽拉着他的手说："别难过，小鸟现在很健康，它一定会过得很好的。"

小明笑了笑说："姐姐，你说得对。能帮助小鸟，我觉得很开心。"

那只小鸟飞走了，但是小明和丽丽永远记得这个春天的故事。他们学会了一件重要的事情：帮助别人，是世界上最快乐的事。

## English Translation

**The Story of the Little Bird**

Spring arrived, and the weather became warmer and warmer. Xiao Ming was an eight-year-old boy who studied hard at school every day. He had an older sister named Lili. Lili was two years older than him, and she was a very kind girl. The sister and brother had a very good relationship and often played together.

One afternoon, after school was over, Xiao Ming walked to the school garden. There were many beautiful flowers in the garden—red ones, yellow ones, white ones—very pretty. Xiao Ming walked and looked at the flowers at the same time. Suddenly, he heard a very small sound. He stopped and listened carefully. The sound was coming from under a big tree.

Xiao Ming slowly walked over and discovered a little bird. The little bird's wing was injured and it couldn't fly. The bird looked both scared and sad. Xiao Ming felt very sorry for it, and he thought: "I must help this little bird."

Xiao Ming carefully placed the little bird in his hand, then slowly walked home. After arriving home, Lili saw the little bird and immediately ran over.

Lili asked him: "Xiao Ming, where did this little bird come from? What happened to it?"

Xiao Ming answered: "Big sister, I found it in the school garden. Its wing is injured, and it can't fly anymore. Can we help it?"

Lili said: "Of course we can! Let's think of a way together."

Lili found a small box and put some soft cloth and cotton inside. She made the box into a warm little nest. Xiao Ming gently placed the little bird into the nest. The little bird was finally not so scared anymore and lay quietly in the warm nest.

Starting from that day, the sister and brother took care of the little bird together every day. In the morning before school, Xiao Ming would first give the little bird water. After coming home from school, Lili would prepare some food for the little bird, such as small insects and rice. Every evening, they would check together whether the little bird's wing had gotten a little better.

One week passed, and the little bird's wing slowly got a bit better. After two weeks, the little bird began to move around in the box. After three weeks, the little bird could already stand up. Xiao Ming and Lili were both very happy.

A few more days passed, and the little bird's wing was completely healed. It flew around the room and looked very happy. Xiao Ming knew that the little bird should return to the sky. Although he was reluctant to part with it, he understood that the little bird belonged to nature.

That day was a sunny day, and the sunlight was wonderful. The sister and brother together brought the little bird to the garden. Xiao Ming opened his hand, the little bird looked at him, then spread its wings and flew toward the blue sky.

Xiao Ming watched the little bird fly far away, and his eyes became a little red. Lili held his hand and said: "Don't be sad. The little bird is very healthy now. It will definitely live well."

Xiao Ming smiled and said: "Big sister, you're right. Being able to help the little bird, I feel very happy."

The little bird flew away, but Xiao Ming and Lili will forever remember this spring's story. They learned an important thing: helping others is the happiest thing in the world.

## Grammatical Points and Analysis

### Comparative Structure: 比 (bǐ)

- **丽丽比他大两岁** (Lìlì bǐ tā dà liǎng suì) — "Lili is two years older than him." The structure is: A + 比 + B + adjective (+ specific amount). This is a standard way to make comparisons.

### Progressive Change: 越来越 (yuè lái yuè)

- **天气变得越来越暖和** (tiānqì biàn de yuè lái yuè nuǎnhuo) — "The weather became warmer and warmer." The pattern 越来越 + adjective expresses that something is continuously increasing in degree.

### Simultaneous Actions: 一边…一边… (yìbiān…yìbiān…)

- **小明一边走一边看花** (Xiǎo Míng yìbiān zǒu yìbiān kàn huā) — "Xiao Ming walked and looked at flowers at the same time." This structure indicates two actions happening simultaneously.

### 把 (bǎ) Construction

- **小明小心地把小鸟放在手上** (Xiǎo Míng xiǎoxīn de bǎ xiǎo niǎo fàng zài shǒu shàng) — "Xiao Ming carefully placed the little bird in his hand." The 把 structure is used to indicate what is done to a specific object: Subject + 把 + Object + Verb + Complement.
- **她把盒子做成了一个温暖的小窝** (Tā bǎ hézi zuò chéng le yí gè wēnnuǎn de xiǎo wō) — "She made the box into a warm little nest."

### Resultative Complements

- **走过去** (zǒu guòqù) — "walked over" (movement toward something)
- **走回家** (zǒu huí jiā) — "walked back home" (movement returning)
- **跑过来** (pǎo guòlái) — "ran over" (movement toward the speaker)
- **飞回了天空** (fēi huí le tiānkōng) — "flew back to the sky"
- **站起来** (zhàn qǐlái) — "stood up"
- **放进了窝里** (fàng jìn le wō lǐ) — "placed into the nest"

### Paired Adjectives: 又…又… (yòu…yòu…)

- **小鸟看起来又害怕又难过** (xiǎo niǎo kàn qǐlái yòu hàipà yòu nánguò) — "The little bird looked both scared and sad." This structure connects two adjectives describing the same subject simultaneously.

### Directional Complements: 来/去 (lái/qù) with Verbs

- **飞来飞去** (fēi lái fēi qù) — "flew back and forth"
- **动来动去** (dòng lái dòng qù) — "moved around restlessly"
- The pattern Verb + 来 + Verb + 去 expresses repeated, back-and-forth actions.

### Expressing Duration and Time Sequence

- **一个星期过去了** (yí gè xīngqī guòqù le) — "One week passed."
- **从那天开始** (cóng nà tiān kāishǐ) — "Starting from that day." 从…开始 marks a time starting point.

### Concession: 虽然…但是… (suīrán…dànshì…)

- **虽然他很舍不得，但是他明白小鸟属于大自然** — "Although he was reluctant to part with it, he understood the little bird belonged to nature." This is the standard way to express "although…but…"

### Modal Verb 会 (huì) for Habitual Actions

- **小明会先给小鸟喂水** (Xiǎo Míng huì xiān gěi xiǎo niǎo wèi shuǐ) — "Xiao Ming would first give the little bird water." Here 会 expresses a habitual or regular action, similar to "would" in English.

### Verb Reduplication: V了V / VV

- **听了听** (tīng le tīng) — "listened for a bit"
- **看了看** (kàn le kàn) — "took a look"
- **笑了笑** (xiào le xiào) — "smiled a little"
- Reduplicating a verb in this way softens the action and suggests it was brief or casual.

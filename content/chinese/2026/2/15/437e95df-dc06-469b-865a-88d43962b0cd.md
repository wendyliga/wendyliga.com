## Grammatical Points and Analysis  
### Verb-Object Phrases:  
- 准备 (zhǔnbèi): prepare  
- 开始 (kāishǐ): start  
- 参加 (cānjiā): participate   

### Resultative Complements:  
- 猜对 (cāiduì): guess correctly  
- 赢得 (yíngdé): win  

### Suggestions Using “先…再…”:  
- Example: 先在家里试试，再参加比赛 (Let’s try it at home first, then join the competition).  

### Expressing Excitement and Emotion Using “觉得” and “感觉”:  
- 觉得 (juéde): feel or think  
- 感觉 (gǎnjué): sense or feel

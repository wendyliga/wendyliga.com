## Grammatical Points and Analysis

### Verb-Object Phrases:
- 做灯笼 (zuò dēnglóng) - to make lanterns. Verb + Object structure.
- 吃元宵 (chī yuánxiāo) - to eat tangyuan (rice balls). Experience verbs with direct objects.
  
### Adverbial Usage with 又...又...:
- 又红又圆 (yòu hóng yòu yuán) - Both red and round. This structure is used to describe multiple characteristics of one thing.

### Time Expressions:
- 一天放学后 (yītiān fàngxué hòu) - One day after school. "一天" sets the time phrase.
- 花了一个小时 (huāle yī gè xiǎoshí) - Spent an hour. 花 + duration is used to express the amount of time spent doing something.

### Resultative Complements:
- 做好了 (zuò hǎo le) - Finished making. 做 (do) + 好 (resultative complement) + 了 (indicates action is completed).

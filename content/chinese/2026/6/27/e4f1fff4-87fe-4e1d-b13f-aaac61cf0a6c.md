## English Translation

Summer arrived, and the weather was very hot. On June 27th, the sun was blazing. Xiaoming and his younger sister Lili worked together with their dad in the small vegetable garden behind the house. The garden was planted with many vegetables, such as cucumbers, tomatoes, and chili peppers. The sun was too hot, and the vegetable leaves all drooped. Xiaoming said, “Dad, I’m worried the vegetables will get sunburned.” Dad said, “Let’s build a shade shelter to protect the vegetables.” Lili said happily, “I’ll help, I’ll get the bamboo poles.” Xiaoming also said, “I’ll get the cloth and rope.” They all set to work together. Dad stuck the bamboo poles into the soil, and Xiaoming and Lili covered a large piece of cloth over the poles and tied it tightly with rope. Very soon, a small shade shelter was set up. The vegetables under the shelter seemed much cooler. Xiaoming said, “I’ll pick the cucumbers; I found two big cucumbers.” Lili said, “I’ll pick the tomatoes; I picked three red tomatoes.” They washed the vegetables clean. Dad said, “For lunch we’ll make smashed cucumber salad and sugar-mixed tomatoes, refreshing and delicious.” Xiaoming and Lili were very happy. Mom was not at home, so they did it themselves. Xiaoming cut the cucumbers, and Lili cut the tomatoes into small pieces. Dad put salt, vinegar, and a little sugar on the cucumbers and mixed them. He sprinkled lots of white sugar over the tomatoes. At lunchtime, they sat in the cool shade beside the shelter, eating the cucumber salad and sugar tomatoes, feeling extremely cool and refreshed. Xiaoming said, “I’m really happy today; the vegetables we grew ourselves taste especially good.” Lili said, “I want to grow even more vegetables in the future.” Dad smiled and said, “Alright, let’s take care of the garden together.” Although summer was hot, being together as a family, and eating the things they grew themselves after working hard, their hearts felt very sweet.

### Verb-Object Phrases:
- **搭遮阳棚** (dā zhēyángpéng) – build a shade shelter

- **摘黄瓜** (zhāi huángguā) – pick cucumbers

- **切黄瓜** (qiē huángguā) – cut cucumbers

- **撒白糖** (sǎ báitáng) – sprinkle white sugar  
- **照顾菜园** (zhàogù càiyuán) – take care of the vegetable garden  

### Resultative Complements:
- **晒坏** (shài huài) – sunburned; “晒” (to sun) + “坏” (bad/damaged) indicating the result of being damaged by the sun.  
- **搭好** (dā hǎo) – successfully set up; “搭” (to build) + “好” (good/well) indicating completion.  
- **绑紧** (bǎng jǐn) – tie tightly; “绑” (to tie) + “紧” (tight) indicating the result of being tight.  
- **洗干净** (xǐ gānjìng) – wash clean; “洗” (to wash) + “干净” (clean) indicating the result of becoming clean.  
- **找到了** (zhǎodào le) – found; “找” (to look for) + “到” (to arrive/achieve) indicating the result of successfully finding.  
- **切成** (qiē chéng) – cut into; “切” (to cut) + “成” (to become) indicating the result of turning into pieces.  
- **凉快多了** (liángkuai duō le) – much cooler; “凉快” (cool) + “多” (much) + “了” indicating a change of state.
